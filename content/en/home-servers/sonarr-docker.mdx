# Sonarr with Docker

```yaml
services:
  sonarr:
    image: lscr.io/linuxserver/sonarr:latest
    environment:
      - PUID=1000
      - PGID=1000
      - TZ=Europe/London
    ports:
      - 8989:8989
    volumes:
      - ./config:/config
      - /srv/media/series:/tv
      - /srv/downloads:/downloads
    restart: unless-stopped
```

Open `http://SERVER_IP:8989` and add indexers and your download client.
